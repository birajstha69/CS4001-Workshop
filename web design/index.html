Introductions

Since 2017, Itahari International College has offered top-tier IT and Business programmes in partnership with London Metropolitan University, UK. It aims to produce globally competitive graduates, making it the first college in Itahari to provide a direct UK university degree. IIC nurtures future leaders and innovators by providing hands-on experiences, real-world projects, and a practical curriculum. Our commitment is evident in our alumni's successful careers and high placement rates. Itahari International College is a flagship institution of Innovate Nepal Group (ING).

Our Infrastructure

Our college has well-developed infrastructure that supports effective learning using the LTW(Lecture, Tutorial & Workshop) learning method and overall student growth. The campus includes spacious classrooms with proper lighting and ventilation, well-equipped science and computer laboratories, a rich library with textbooks and reference materials, and a clean, peaceful environment. The college also provides facilities such as a playground, seminar hall, canteen, and reliable internet access. These modern facilities help students learn comfortably and improve their academic performance.
